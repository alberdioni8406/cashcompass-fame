Ongoing maintenance, new ecosystem listings, ambassador profiles, and the AI guide — all built and hosted free of charge for the BCH community.

CompassPay Infrastructure

Serverless payment tools, live CoinPaprika price feeds, CashAddr validation, and QR generation — keeping BCH payments practical and accessible.
